Slide size: 1280x720.
Background: deep ink (#101827) for chapter/architecture slides, warm off-white (#F7F5EF) for evidence and matrix slides.
Typography: Aptos Display for claims and section headers; Aptos for body; Aptos Mono for code/logs.
Palette: ink #101827, paper #F7F5EF, white #FFFFFF, soft #6B7280, line #D9D3C7, green #2E7D5B, amber #D58A24, blue #2563EB, red #B42318. Green means working flow, amber means external dependency or warning, blue means API/automation, red means failure/risk.
Diagram grammar: rectangular nodes with 8px radius max by convention; equal-role nodes share size; lines are thin and direct.
Connector grammar: direction shown only where data/control actually moves; connector labels stay attached to the line.
Container grammar: boxes represent real modules/layers/stages, not decoration.
Footer: source/evidence label left, slide number right.
Title/kicker: small all-caps kicker, one claim title per slide.
Data labels: direct labels, no heavy legends.
Brand cues: FoodTrack wordmark as text only; no fake logo.
Allowed layouts: cover with metric rail, system map, module matrix, screenshot spread, sequence flow, factor matrix, log terminal, pipeline lane, roadmap.
Banned motifs: decorative gradients, mascots, fake badges, crowded prose cards, unsupported claims.
